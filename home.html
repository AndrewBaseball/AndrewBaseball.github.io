FILTR

FILTR is a company where we want to change the world, and make it a cleaner place. FILTR is a product where we use some of the best technology to extract Carbon Dioxide from ordinary gases and trap it, to have our air cleaner. Our company slows down the rate of global warming, while making our products, not only affordable, but meaningful. FILTR came together when we met at a summercamp in Palo Alto, CA. All four of the founders showed a certain passion for climate change and wanting to stop it, so we came up with a revolutionary idea, a carbon capture filter that could fit on a car or smokestack. Then, the FILTR was born, created in 2019, The FILTR has dominated over other climate change companies just by making their products affordable and portable, without being a pain in the ***.
